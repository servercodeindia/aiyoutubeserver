Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

YouTubeServer.com respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. No registration, login, or account creation required. The only data processed is the video URL you submit, used solely to serve the download.

2. Cookies

We may use minimal, essential cookies for functionality (e.g., language preference). No tracking or advertising cookies.

3. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics for general traffic patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service processes video URLs from third-party platforms (YouTube, Facebook, etc.). We do not store downloaded videos. Downloads stream directly from the source to your device.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures. Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal risk of data breach.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children.
